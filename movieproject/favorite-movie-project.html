Story

This movie is about how 6 nerds become Superheros and saved the world from the evil Calico. They were first met up at the Robotics University were Tadashi does his work. He was working on a project that is so magnificent that he can cure and help anyone. The big Marshmallow named Baymax is a squishy and smart robot. He was programmed to help the sick or injured. This would soon be used in the future. All of the nerds there are all striving for the future!
